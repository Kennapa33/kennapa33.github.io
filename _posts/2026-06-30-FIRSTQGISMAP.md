After graduation, I have been focusing part of my time on expanding my GIS skillset by learning to transfer my skills from ArcGIS to QGIS, a free, open-source alternative. Following my practicum project, I am continuing my focus on the Santa Cruz River and nearby systems. Today I worked on mapping land use in the three US counties that Santa Cruz flows through: Santa Cruz County, Pima County, and Pinal County. This small map I created shows the 2025 Annual NLCD values for these three counties. My future plans are to map the NLCD for previous years in these three counties to show land development changes and then preform a statistical analysis.
